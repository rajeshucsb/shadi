Bita & Rajesh


			Under the glorious sun,
			A child was born,
			And the name Rajesh was coined. 
			
			At the first rays of dawn,
			First born in the clan she was unique & bonita,
			They named her Bita.
			
			Their paths crossed in Santa Barbara,
			Where they both went to college,
			They met and became good friends.
			
			It was time for him to graduate and move to far away lands,
			But the greater forces had their own plans,
			Economic crisis hit and he stayed in Santa Barbara.
		    
			They hung out together,
			Went ballroom dancing, played board games and ate,
			And then he asked her on a date.
		    
			The rest of the story is history my friends,
			They are getting married on 6th of April 2013,
			And are kindly requesting your presence.
